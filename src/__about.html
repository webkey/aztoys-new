About Us

Company and Brand

AZ Toys is a new division of AZ Group LLC, the international group of companies with offices in Europe and the USA. We are a dynamic and fast growing company with extensive experience in the sphere of children's products. We have been on the global market for over 20 years, and we are well-known for our educational books, e-applications, and interactive toys for children. Our guiding principles are high quality, modern design and unlimited educational content for all kids!

Our Toys

Designed for children from 2 to 8, and selected with great care, our toys inspire fun learning. As a result a boring process of learning turns into a captivating and exciting game! We provide a great variety of smart interactive toys: from tablets, smart phones, kids’ pads, musical pianos, microphones to educational cards, smart pens, bright and unusual puzzles! Our toys have something special even for the most demanding customers!

Our Technologies

Creating our toys we use the most advanced technologies. We carefully examine and analyze children’s needs and interests along with their parents’ preferences. Our company meets high requirements of modern competitive market. All of our products comply with current manufacturing and safety standards. We are always looking for new ways to turn educational process into a fascinating game bringing fun and joy!

Our Team

Our company has a team of highly skilled specialists, who are aimed at creating and inventing unique and advanced toys. Pedagogues, methodologists, illustrators, designers, actors, narrators, sound supervisors and engineers take part in the realization of our ambitious projects. We thoroughly study the modern toy market, considering different cultural aspects, and create an adaptive product, unique for every single market! Our extensive experience lets us create a complete, high-quality product. We feel inspired by your children! We are developing and growing up together with them!

Our Uniqueness

It’s important for toys to be age appropriate, stimulate children’s imagination, encourage interaction and develop their skills. Our toys are designed to make it fun for children to grow and learn through active and creative play. Our main aim is to make children excited about learning, and that is why ‘playing to get smart’ is our key principle. We believe the children’s toys have to inspire as well as entertain! For those who are looking for unique educational toys, we have a great number of options to offer. Make your learning fun with AzToys!

Achievements

Our products are translated into more than 40 languages; and our partners from over 62 countries around the globe have already appreciated the originality, wide range, and high quality we offer. We participate in international toy fairs annually, improving and enriching our assortment, demonstrating novelties and presenting our latest solutions! Our company is a permanent participant of the most famous toy fairs: the International Toy Fair in Nuremberg and New York International Toy Fair. We consider our greatest achievement is to be popular and well-recognized among children and their parents!

Cooperation

We invite you to discover a large variety of our products, and develop a cooperative relationship with us so that you can share our success. The policy of our cooperation has been always based on flexibility! We are open to diverse and mutually advantageous cooperation as a distributor of our already existing products, OEM, ODM. Cooperation with us is easy, fruitful, long-term and beneficial! We make our business a pleasure!

Our Strategy

Liberal and competitive pricing policy is the core of our business strategy. We offer unique educational toys at affordable prices! We independently create the design of our toys and their packages as well as provide multilanguage support. These advantages allow us to offer different variants of packs and be adaptive to any culture and market. We care about your expenses!
